About Me

I fell in love with code in 2002 when I learned HTML and used it to create three websites for American Express. As a corporate communicator, I loved the creativity of creating user-friendly websites to communicate information to employees and the ability to keep the content fresh and accurate by updating the website daily.

I spent over 10 years at American Express creating websites from code and managing the site content. Each of the websites I created ranked in the top five percent most visited American Express sites.

When the company changed to a CMS platform, I gained five years of experience creating and managing websites on Interwoven and SharePoint content management systems.
