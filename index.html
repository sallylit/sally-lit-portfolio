About Me

I fell in love with HTML code during my 11-year tenure as a communications specialist for American Express. I designed and built several user-friendly websites that captured the attention of employees. I managed the content of the pages through daily updates and conducted user experience testing twice a year to keep the pages enticing to the employees. Each of the websites I created consistently ranked in the top five percent of the most visited American Express sites.

In 2017, I returned to school to expand on my web designer and development skills to learn HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and React along with Adobe Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator. That's what I love about the tech world - there's always new skills to learn!
